About

Fusing industry needs with advanced technology

Simplot, a leader in innovation and ingenuity, has developed FŪSN™, a safe source of nitrate that is better for the environment and packed with agronomic benefits for plants and soil. It is a new ammonium nitrate fertilizer made less detonable by fusing ammonium nitrate with ammonium sulfate.

Simplot’s FŪSN (26-0-0-14s) provides growers the ability to improve farm productivity with a fertilizer that is easy to use and allows an improved bottom line with clear agronomic benefits. It’s just one more way Simplot is improving farm productivity and reducing operational impact through advanced technology.
