Benefits

Benefits for your plants – and your bottom line.

The technology behind FŪSN™ revitalizes a proven nutrient that your plants want and need. Simplot has created a safe, ammonium nitrate-based fertilizer that delivers powerful agronomic benefits, including optimized plant uptake potential and higher crop yields. Simplot’s FŪSN is a fused sulfate-nitrate molecule dry, granular fertilizer that delivers more dual nitrate and ammonium nitrogen to the plant and improves crop nutrient release. In multiple crop tests, FŪSN delivered equal or superior crop yields and quality over other combinations of nitrogen and sulfur fertilizers

FŪSN is also a socially responsible fertilizer that decreases the volatilization of surface applied nitrogen. It also improves operational efficiency over ammonium nitrate and is easy to store and use. FŪSN is an ammonium nitrate fertilizer you can count on.

FŪSN is a great example of 4R Nutrient Stewardship®

Applying the right source of nutrients at the right rate at the right time and in the right place is an efficient use of resources and a worldwide accepted best management practice for fertilizers.
